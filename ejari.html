Ejari Services

Do you want EJARI services in UAE? Albarq Documents Clearing Service is ready here to help you. The Real Estate Regulatory Agency (RERA) created the Ejari online registration system, which mandates that all lease or rental agreements in Dubai be documented there. Lease contract attestation and cancellation, ownership certificate applications, and the issuance of all types of plans are meticulously handled in this. The EJARI electronic registration web service is designed to meet the requirements of the law and RERA’s vision and mission to establish a robust regulatory system for the rental market and protect the rights of everyone involved. Albarq Documents Clearing Service, can assist you in registering the tenancy contract and comply with RERA. Expert professionals at Albarq Document Clearing service is always ready to guide you and help you in the registering process.
